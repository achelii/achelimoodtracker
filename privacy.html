Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Acheli Mood Tracker (“Acheli”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data (if you sign in): user ID and profile data from Apple/Google sign-in.
  • Mood data: mood entries, notes/reviews, selected topics/submoods, thinking patterns, favorites, and optional location label/coordinates.
  • Photos: optional mood photos. Depending on your settings and plan, photos may be stored locally or uploaded to cloud storage.
  • AI usage data: prompts and responses used for AI chat/analysis features.
  • Purchase data: subscription/token purchase status from App Store transactions.
  • Device/technical data: crash/performance and notification token data needed for app functionality and push notifications.
  • Advertising data: ad interactions for AdMob (for free-tier users).

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide core app features (mood logging, analytics, social sharing, AI features).
  • Sync your data across devices when signed in.
  • Send notifications (if enabled).
  • Process purchases and entitlements.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and safety.
  • Show ads in free-tier experience.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We use service providers such as Firebase, Apple, Google, and AdMob to operate app features.
  • Shared mood entries are visible only according to your in-app sharing settings.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

4. AI Features

AI outputs are generated automatically and may be inaccurate. They are for reflection/support only and are not medical, legal, or professional advice.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You can delete entries and request account/data deletion.

6. Your Choices

  • Use the app without mandatory sign-in for basic use.
  • Control sharing settings for moods/photos.
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Request deletion of your account/data.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures, but no system is 100% secure.

9. International Processing

Your data may be processed on servers outside your country.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes are effective when posted on this page.
